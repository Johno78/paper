Quick reference

Know your fold

Dispensers and towels are matched by fold, not just brand — here's what H2, H3, V-fold and W-fold actually mean before you order.

H2

Multifold

Interfolded so one towel pulls the next into place. Compact, efficient dispensing.

H3

Singlefold

One flat sheet per pull. Simple mechanism, works in most standard dispensers.

V

V-fold

Sheets nest in a V shape for a slimmer stack — a common fit for narrow dispensers.

W

W-fold

A deeper folded sheet giving more surface area per towel — often used where absorbency matters most.
